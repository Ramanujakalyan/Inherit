Why should I upgrade my deal?

With Madzz you have a powerful FREE feature that allows you to list your deal as a basic listing for FREE.

Upgrading your Deal Listing enables your deal to standout and attract attention. You are bound to get a greater number of viewers and traffic. In addition, upgrading your deal also enables you to get deeper business insights about users who have visited your store, saved your deals etc. Also, there are few featured spots which means you don't lose the advantage of a Featured Listing. We recommend you use the carousel listing when you want to attract a larger set of customers and across multiple locations.
